Feeding the Newest New Yorkers: Rethink Food's Restaurant Partners and Shelter Sites

More than 180,000 asylum seekers have arrived in New York City in the last 18 months.

Rethink Food is a New York City-based nonprofit with a mission of combating food insecurity.

Currently, Rethink is contracted with the city government to provide three meals a day at various temporary asylum seeker shelter sites in New York City.

Rethink supplies these meals by partnering with local restaurants. The restaurants cook the meals and deliver them to the shelter sites, with Rethink providing organizational and logistical support.

This map shows selected asylum seeker shelter sites and the locations of Rethink's restaurant partners. This information will allow Rethink to plan its delivery processes more efficiently.
